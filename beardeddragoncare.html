Bearded Dragon Care Guide

Temperature and UV

Basking surface temperature of 113F (45C) read using an infra-red heat gun.
10% to 12% UVB

Enclosure

Use a wooden vivarium in order to maintain high temperatures. Measuring 4ft wide X 2ft deep X 2ft high which is about 95 gallons.

Diet

Bearded dragons are omnivores and require a diet of invertebrates, fruits, vegetables and leafy greens.

Safe vegetables, fruits and greens

  • Spring greens/collard greens
  • Leek
  • Squash
  • Curly Kale (in moderation)
  • Rocket
  • Watercress
  • Pepper
  • Radish
  • Green Beans
  • Mango (in moderation)
  • Grapes (in moderation)
  • Banana (in modetation)
  • Blueberries (in moderation)

Safe invertebrates

  • Dubia roaches
  • Locust
  • Crickets
  • Morio worms (in moderation)
